Marzipan

Recipe description

Marzipan is a sweet almond-based treat, smooth in texture and rich in flavor. Made from ground almonds and sugar, it has a delicate nutty taste that pairs beautifully with its natural sweetness. Popular across Europe, marzipan is often used for candies, cake decorations, and festive desserts.

This simple homemade version is soft, pliable, and delicious on its own or shaped into small figures. It can also be dipped in chocolate or used as a filling for pastries. Its versatility and melt-in-the-mouth texture make it a favorite for both casual snacking and special occasions.

Ingredients list

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1-2 tsp water (if needed)
