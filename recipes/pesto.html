Pesto recipe

Description

Pesto is a traditional Italian sauce made from fresh basil,
garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil.

It has a rich, aromatic flavor and is commonly used
with pasta, bread, or as a topping for various dishes.

Ingridients

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Pine nuts
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Preparing

  1. Wash and dry the fresh basil leaves.
  2. Peel the garlic cloves.
  3. Blend basil, garlic, and pine nuts together.
  4. Add grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Slowly pour in olive oil while blending.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
