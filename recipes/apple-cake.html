Apple cake

Recipe description

Apple pie is a timeless dessert loved for its flaky crust and sweet, spiced apple filling. The combination of tender apples, sugar, and cinnamon creates a comforting flavor that feels both homely and festive. Its golden crust and fragrant aroma make it a favorite at family gatherings and holidays.

This pie can be served warm or cold, on its own or with extras like vanilla ice cream or whipped cream. Its balance of sweetness and slight tartness makes it versatile and satisfying, a dessert that brings people together around the table.

Ingredients

  • 2 and half cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold, cubed)
  • half cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 medium apples (peeled, sliced)
  • half cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 egg (for brushing)

Preparing

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 C (350 F).
  2. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, butter, and water. Chill for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough and place it into a pie dish.
  4. In a bowl, combine apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice.
  5. Spread the apple mixture evenly into the pie crust.
  6. Cover with the top crust or lattice, sealing the edges.
  7. Brush with beaten egg for a golden finish.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
