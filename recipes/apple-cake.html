Apple cake

Recipe description

Apple pie is a timeless dessert loved for its flaky crust and sweet, spiced apple filling. The combination of tender apples, sugar, and cinnamon creates a comforting flavor that feels both homely and festive. Its golden crust and fragrant aroma make it a favorite at family gatherings and holidays.

This pie can be served warm or cold, on its own or with extras like vanilla ice cream or whipped cream. Its balance of sweetness and slight tartness makes it versatile and satisfying, a dessert that brings people together around the table.
