Miso soup

Description

Miso soup is one of the most popular traditional Japanese dishes. It is made with a flavorful base called dashi, mixed with miso paste, and usually served as a warm and comforting starter.

This light and savory soup is often paired with rice and other side dishes. Soft tofu, seaweed, and green onions are common ingredients that give miso soup its unique taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • Dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • Tofu
  • Wakame seaweed
  • Green onions

Preparing

  1. Prepare dashi stock in a pot.
  2. Cut tofu into small cubes.
  3. Add wakame seaweed to the hot dashi.
  4. Stir in miso paste until it dissolves completely.
  5. Add tofu cubes and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle with chopped green onions before serving.
